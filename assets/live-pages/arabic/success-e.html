Hussein Sadiq

The engineering research Hussein Sadiq is conducting at Monash University could guide the very shape of Iraq's cities. And the teaching style he has witnessed during his time as a PhD student could shape the way his compatriots learn.

Hussein hopes to return to his native Iraq and inspire a method of "critical thinking and independent learning" in classrooms, rather than teaching students the right answers.

"The plan is to go back home and transfer my knowledge ... through work, teaching and consultation," he said. "That's how you build a community."
