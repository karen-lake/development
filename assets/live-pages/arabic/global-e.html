World-renowned research

Our ambitious research brings about real and positive change to people all over the world.

We have been at the forefront of:

  • Numerous scientific ventures based on research conducted at the Australian Synchrotron, which adjoins our Clayton campus
  • Nanoscience
  • Biomedicine
  • Using stem cell research to revolutionise treatments for conditions like Parkinson's disease and spinal cord injury
  • Aeronautical engineering, having partnered with Boeing and the Commonwealth Scientific and Industrial Research Organisation (CSIRO) to fast-track the design and construction of a new generation of super-lightweight and efficient passenger planes
  • Pioneering in-vitro fertilisation (IVF) technology
  • Combating the world's most common viruses such as malaria and influenza through drug development
  • Helping reduce road fatalities through ground-breaking accident research
  • Preventing and treating obesity
  • Investigating ways medicine can help the adult body repair and regenerate damaged tissues and organs

Global success

Our ever-expanding global alumni network now numbers well over 200,000 people. Many of these former students continue to excel in their chosen fields. Many have found success at multinational companies.

Some hold high-level leadership positions in government and non-government organisations. All have been equipped to make a difference to the communities in which they work and live.

Group of Eight

Monash University is part of the Group of Eight, a coalition of prestigious Australian universities, renowned for their focus on research and the high standard of education they provide.

The universities that make up the Group of Eight collectively produce more than 70 per cent of all basic research conducted at Australia's universities.

Monash is the only Group of Eight university in Melbourne to offer double-degrees, which allow students to select different discipline electives, tailoring their course to suit their talents and interests.

Education Services for Overseas Students (ESOS)

Monash University strictly abides by the Australian legislative framework known as ESOS.

ESOS has a long-held reputation for protecting Australia's standing as a provider of outstanding education to overseas students. It sets out guidelines (and minimum standards) that ensure education providers like Monash offer high-quality tuition and an excellent level of student care.
