Change of Preference registration prize give away

Register to attend and you'll automatically go into the draw to win one of four iPhones and four iPads*. Refer to www.monash.edu for terms and conditions.

This competition is free to enter. Everyone who registers online to attend the 2012 Change of Preference Expo at Monash University Caulfield campus to be held on Saturday 17 December will be automatically entered into the draw. Employees of Monash University and their immediate families are not eligible to enter. There are four iPads (Wifi 64GB) and four iPhones (16GB) with a total approximate retail value of $4,712.00 to be given away. *Apple is not a participant in or sponsor of this promotion.

Eight winners will be drawn randomly and the prizes will be awarded in draw order (ie first four winners drawn will receive an iPad each; second four winners will receive an iPhone each).

The winners will be notified by email or phone and the names of the winners will be published online by Wednesday 21 December. Entry is limited to one entry per person and incomplete or multiple entries will be deemed invalid. The competition closes Saturday 17 December at 2pm. The prize draw will be conducted on Monday 19 December at 11am Building H, Caulfield Campus.
