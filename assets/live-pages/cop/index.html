What's next

Step 1: Follow your career dream

Remember to choose a course that interests you and will help you get to the career of your dreams. Use our ATAR tool to find out what courses are available to you. Remember the clearly in ATAR's published are the result of last year's admissions. The clearly in ATAR can move up or down a little bit each year. Also remember students with special consideration may get an offer with an ATAR below this.

Step 2: Do your research on courses

If you are thinking about adding new courses, remember to carefully check if there are pre-requisites subjects or if there are special requirements you have to meet. For example, attending an interview.

Step 3: Look at all pathways that lead to your Monash course

If you didn't get the ATAR you expected remember Monash has numerous pathway programs to get you to the Monash course of your dreams.

Step 4: Check out uni life at Monash

There's more to a course than just the ATAR requirement! Check the facilities, transport, clubs and societies and more that are available to you at Monash.

Step 5: Come see us!

If you're still unsure about your preferences come see us at the Change of Preference Expo and make sure you know about the University that offers you a world of possibilities.
