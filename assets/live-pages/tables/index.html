Your results – when and how

How to access your unit results

Results by SMS

You can register to receive your unit results early, for semesters one and two, by SMS on your mobile phone. You need to do this each semester.

Log into my.monash to register. We’ll SMS you your results the night before the official result release date each semester. So keep an eye on your phone from the early evening onwards. There is no cost for this service.

Results in the Web Enrolment System (WES)

You can access and print your unit results in the Web Enrolment System (WES) once your results are released.

  1. Log into WES using your Monash account username and password.
  2. In the Course Progression menu, click Results/Unofficial academic record.

You can also access and print your previous results at anytime in WES.

If you need help with unexpected results, see the related links section at bottom of this page.
